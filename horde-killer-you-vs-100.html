    Horde Killer: You vs 100

    Horde Killer: You vs 100 is a chaotic survival game where you face a vast horde of zombies determined to kill. Shoot them, blow them to pieces - do whatever it takes to avoid being mauled. Buy a range of new weapons and outfits for an explosively stylish apocalypse scenario.

    Orange Origins

    Who are these orange-tinted glass chaps? They were once ordinary people, but they drank a strange liquid that turned them orange and gave them a hankering for human flesh. Now there’s a full-blown apocalypse somehow, and all you can do is shoot them down relentlessly for sport while staying alive.

    How to Play

    Run and shoot relentlessly to survive

    Running, screaming, and shooting are core elements of the game. Use the sheer running speed of your legs combined with the various obstacles to create distance between you and the horde. Then execute trigger time and explosives - kill them all.

    Take a shopping trip to the armory

    There are many ways to kill an enraged orange zombie. The shop features a range of weapons you can discharge on the swarm, from machine guns and pistols to excellent explosive devices. And, for a few seconds of your time, you can get ultimate weapons like the RPG and minigun to inflict absolute carnage for one round only.

    Look pretty while slaying

    Sure, it’s the apocalypse, but style still matters. Horde Killer: You vs 100 features an extensive range of character customization options. There are various bodies, heads, hairstyles, beards, and eyes to wear that ensure you're dressed to impress while pulling the trigger. It’s best to find an outfit you don’t mind being splattered with zombie guts.

    Features

    • Battle with a chaotic horde of 100 zombies
    • Use bonus weapons to absolutely obliterate zombies fast
    • Buy a range of new guns and explosives in the weapon shop
    • Extensive character customizations for that chic zombie slayer aesthetic

    Release Date

    The game was released in April 2022 and can be played exclusively on CrazyGames!

    Developer

    Horde Killer: You vs 100 is developed by GoGoMan, who also created other popular games such as Time Shooter 2, Time Shooter 3, and Funny Battle Simulator 2.

    Controls

    • WASD / ZQSD = move
    • Space = roll / jump over / climb
    • Left-click = shoot
    • Right-click = aim
    • Mouse wheel / 1-5 = change weapons
    • G = grenade
    • R = reload
