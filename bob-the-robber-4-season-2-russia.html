    Bob The Robber 4 Russia

    In this instalment of the popular game series, Bob the Robber 4: Season 2, Bob is back again one more time and ready to take on the world. This time he sets his eyes on Russia. His goal is to steal every valuable item he can find here. Are you ready to join him on this adventure?

    You know what they say: You should do what you love. Well, Bob loves stealing. He has even become world-wide famous for his deeds. He's as quiet as a mouse and nobody seems him coming. So, all those who oppose him, you better beware! But since he's a stranger to this country, he might need some help. Your objective is to complete each level by stealing the trophy and running away without getting caught. But there are many dangers you need to overcome. Beware of big and strong guards since you can't knock them down. Don't get caught on security cameras, knock down the other guards and open locked doors that stand between you and treasure. Click on the items you see around to steal more money and buy new stuff that will help you along the way from the in-game shop. Oh, just a tip before we finally begin. You can always hide in the shadows if a guard is nearby. Good luck!

    Bob may be done here but he needs help in other countries as well. Check out our collection of Bob the Robber games and join him on the fun.

    Controls
